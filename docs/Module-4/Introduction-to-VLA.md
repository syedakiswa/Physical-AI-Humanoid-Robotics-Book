# Introduction to Vision-Language-Action (VLA)

Vision-Language-Action (VLA) is the next generation of robotics where the robot:

1. **Listens** (voice input),
2. **Understands** (language model),
3. **Sees** (computer vision), and
4. **Acts** (robotic control).

### Why VLA?

Traditional robots follow pre-programmed instructions.  
VLA robots understand human commands like:

- “Pick the bottle from the table”
- “Clean the room”
- “Bring me the red cup”

This module shows how robots convert **voice → language → perception → action**.
